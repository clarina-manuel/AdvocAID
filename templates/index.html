AdvocAID

An AI-Powered Platform Empowering Women to Advocate for Themselves in the Workplace

AdvocAID provides real-world, workplace scenarios like asking for a raise, balancing a challenging workload, or negotiating deadlines, to which users can respond and practice their conversational and self-advocacy skills. Using sentiment analysis, AdvocAID evaluates tone and confidence, offering constructive feedback and actionable tips to refine their approach. Users can retry scenarios, improving their skills in a supportive environment.
